Printable automotive logos by Canva

Just like other businesses, a car-related company also needs a professional logo. After coming up with the right business name, you need to create a logo for your storefront and your marketing collateral. It has to be memorable and unique to your business.

Use Canva to create your logo the easy way. Our automotive logo templates are free and customizable for your business needs. These premade professional designs are ready for you.

We have dozens of automotive repair logo ideas that you can choose from for your brand. Scroll through our library and look for a template that reflects the look you want to have, whether you want something that shows some gears or has a minimalist feel.

Once you’ve found an automotive company logo that’s close to the brand you want to create, you can click on it and start personalizing.

Canva’s platform is intuitive and easy to use. Being creative and customizing your chosen automotive logo design is easy. Even without an expensive design software or a graphic design background, you can create a professional-looking logo in minutes.

Begin by swapping our text guides with your business name and tagline. You can also change the font from hundreds of font options available for you. If you want to change parts of the layout, make the most out of our comprehensive collection. We have thousands of free and premium assets from our stock library that you can use. Use the search box and type the automotive element you need to find icons, lines, shapes, illustrations, graphics, photos, videos, and more for your custom logo.

If you want a fresh set of eyes to comment and work on your logos, you can share a unique link for viewing or editing. You can collaborate with your colleagues in a breeze and finalize your design faster. Then, you have the option to download your logo to use for the promotional materials that you can make and print with Canva. In no time, everyone will associate your car business with your eye-catching automotive logo.
